Mushroom Udon Stir Fry from the gram

main page

Ingredients

  • regular soy sauce
  • dark soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
    • pepper
  • oil
  • garlic
  • green onions
  • napa cabage
  • udon
  • water
  • enoki mushrooms

Instructions

  1. in a bowl combine regular soy sause, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesseme oil, black pepper
  2. in a wok add water and boil frozen udon until lossened
  3. strain imediatly
  4. in another wok add oil, garlic, green onions and cook for 15 seconds
  5. add king oyster mushrooms and enoki mushrooms on med heat and cook until soft
  6. add udon and sause and napa cabage
  7. cook until cabage has wilted
  8. serve and enjoy
