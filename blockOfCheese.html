Block o Cheese

main page

Ingredients

  • Wheel of cheese
  • Hill
  • Transportation
  • Strainer

Instructions

  1. Wash your hands 5 times and then spin around in a circle
  2. Go to your local cheese wheel factory that is in your town
  3. Buy a wheel of cheese
  4. Take your wheel of cheese home
  5. Roll it down your nearest hill within 2 hours
  6. Cut the wheel in half
  7. Take that half and melt it in a pot
  8. Pour that now melted cheese through a strainer into the drain
  9. Take the other half and cut it into a cube shape
  10. There you go you have your block of cheese
