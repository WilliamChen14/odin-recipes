Dollar Soup

main page

Ingredients

  • Dollar bill
  • A manager named bill
  • A paper bill they have to pay
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Wash your hands 5 times and then run in a infinity shape
  2. Carefully place 7 dollar bills into a pot with 2 gallons of distilled water
  3. Bring to a boil and then turn the heat to medium heat to let simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Turn down the heat to low and let it cook overnight for at least 12 hours
  5. Carefully scoop 1/7th of the soup into a bowl and a dollar from the pot
  6. Serve with optional garnish of cilantro or the dollar coin
